FreeBSD

FreeBSD (a successor of 386BSD) is a Unix-like OS, mostly for the Intel and Alpha platforms and is most up-to-date among BSDs regarding device drivers. Ports to the UltraSparc, PowerPC, and AMD-64 platforms are underway.
