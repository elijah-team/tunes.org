Moscow

Julian R. Hall's Moscow MIA is about implementing an OS as libraries of reusable and replacable objects. Nothing has been released but design documents, now unaccessible, but a side effect of Julian's work on tools that would later help him realize his goal has been the development of the Netwide Assembler(NASM), perhaps the best free assembler available.
