RISC OS

RISC OS is the nifty OS that runs on (defunct?) Acorn's ARM-based RISC PC (now RiscStation). Remember how they were the first to introduce inexpensive RISC technology into consumer electronics to compete with the usual bloated designs?

See also The RISC OS Knowledge Base, RISCOS.org and RISCOS.NET.
