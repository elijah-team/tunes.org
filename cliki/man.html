Man

The term for some fragile machine that takes several tens of years to manufacture, with a low success rate, and only seconds to get definitely out-of-order.

The most powerful and most versatile machine known to date, however; actually, the only one known to can pass the Turing test (by definition).

The TUNES Project uses this term to refer to the collective whole of all individuals.
