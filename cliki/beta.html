Beta

An OO programming language from those who invented Simula. It is the latest (1978) advance in OO programming.

  • WWW Site

  • Mjolner, a proprietary implementation
  • GBETA, a free interpreter of a generalised version of BETA
  • MetaBETA, a meta-level architecture for allowing dynamic typing in BETA.
