Chorus

A proprietary microkernel OS, rival of Mach and a RTOS, (now acquired by Sun Microsystems) for telecommunication devices (cellphones, etc.) and network appliances (webphones, set top boxes, etc...).

  • ChorusOS, out-of-production.
  • Chorus Operating System Open Source from Sun Microsystems Laboratories.
