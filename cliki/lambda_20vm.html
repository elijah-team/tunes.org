Lambda VM

Lambda calculus is not only an abstract calculus, but an Abstract VM: a kind of calculus of very basic abstraction. It was invented even before Turing Machines, also as a universal computational framework. Check pages and books about functional programming, functional programming languages, computer science laboratories, theoretical computer science research, etc, for more about lambda calculus.

A few pointers:

  • See the series of "Lambda, The Ultimate FOO" articles. In the original ones by GLS, FOO={ Imperative | Declarative | Goto | Opcode }. They are available among other classic hits from MIT AI lab publications.
