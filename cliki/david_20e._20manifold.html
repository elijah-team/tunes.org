David E. Manifold

David E. Manifold is a person, a contributor and a member of this project. He also contributed bespin to TUNES.

He has offered his time, hardware and internet connection to the TUNES project, providing us with constantly updated, configured, working, well-administered hardware and software server infrastructure, namely, bespin.

His homepage is at tril.tunes.org.
