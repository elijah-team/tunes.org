Open

A political term for a system that is ever subject to dynamic enhancement, to external suggestions, whose development team is ever awake and open-minded (i.e. receptive to arguments or ideas: impartial). The only meaningful criteria for an open mind, besides immediate interest (see expediency), is rationality and selection through fair competition (see tradition, Liberalism).
