Haiku

Haiku is a desktop OS that will be binary-compatible with BeOS R5.

Goals:

  • Administration free
  • Easy to use (e.g.: less options, better defaults)
  • Free and Open
  • Fast even on older hardware
  • Powerful enough for the most complex uses
  • Exciting to use

They have an interesting subproject called Glass Elevator that gathers new ideas for future releases: see their mailinglist.
