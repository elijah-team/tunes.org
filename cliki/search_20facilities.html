Search Facilities

Various kinds of Search Facilities:

  • Algorithms and Data Structures Research & Reference Material.
  • BUBL Information Service, in particular BUBL LINK / 5:15 a Catalogue of Selected Internet Resources, covering all academic subject areas.
  • The Collected Algorithms (CALGO) of the ACM.
  • DataCompression.info.
  • DBLP Bibliography.
  • Dictionary of Algorithms and Data Structures at NIST.
  • The KLI Theory Lab - Artificial Intelligence, Computing a scientific database.
  • MIT AI Lab Publications.
  • Numerical Algorithms Group (NAG) Website Search Facilities.
  • SEL-HPC Article Archive: the London & South-East centre for High Performance Computing archive.
  • Very Large Data Base Endowment Inc. (VLDB Endowment).
  • Wayback Machine at Internet Archive: useful to search MIA pages or old versions of pages still on-line.
  • The WWW Virtual Library: Computer Science.
