Algebraic Specification

The means for using various types of algebras to specify and verify the behavior and semantics of (often distributed or concurrent) programs.

  • The Common Framework Initiative, working on a Common Algrebraic Specification Language.
  • Some executable specification languages, including CafeOBJ and Maude.
