Inferno

A networked OS from Vita Nuova and its Limbo language, successor of Plan9 (from Bell Labs), the system that reinvented scoping for coarse-grained objects. My, after they ignored it for 25 years, which resulted in the whole world having systems deprived of it, the guys from Bell Labs rediscover it. All praise Hell-lahs! -- A N Other.
