AROS

AROS, the Amiga Research OS project, lead by Aaron Digulla is rewriting a free software clone of AmigaOS from scratch, hoping to fix bugs and legal problems with the original OS, and finally port it away from the deceased (until further notice) Amiga hardware platform to the i386 PC (already complete) and other platforms (PPC, Alpha, whatever).
