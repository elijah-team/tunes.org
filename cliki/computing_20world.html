Computing World

The term for the concept encompassing all computers in the world, that are considered as being running different versions of a common Operating System, and linked to a global network. In fact, using our OS definition, the global OS is the greatest common divisor of all existing systems (that is, not much), and even computers remotely linked to an actual network through weekly, monthly, or yearly floppies is considered as being on the global network.
