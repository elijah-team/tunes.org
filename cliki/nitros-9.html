NitrOS-9

NitrOS-9 is basically a reconstruction of OS-9 for the 6809 Tandy CoCo3, but refined for the Hitachi 6309 compatible CPU. Development group is pretty small, 'cause the hardware is pretty rare these days, but it is a fascinatingly loyal bunch of dedicated OS hackers.
