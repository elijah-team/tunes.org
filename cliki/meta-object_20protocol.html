Meta-Object Protocol

A term for, simply stated, a set of rules for manipulating and communicating with meta-objects. In other words, it's a metaprogramming library, or even a language in its own right, which the Lisp family of languages provides rather simply with the language itself. Or you could say that it's the implicit meaning of meta-objects: what the user can't control about meta-objects in systems which do not allow Tunes-level (system-wide) reflection.
