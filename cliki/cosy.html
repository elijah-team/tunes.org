CoSy

CoSy (Coherent Systems) is an environment, by Bob Armstrong; it was based on APL, then on K and now on Forth.

It is somewhat interesting because of its Textual User Interface (TUI) resemblance with Oberon, The Humane Environment and other similar systems.
