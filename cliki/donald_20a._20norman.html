Donald A. Norman

A person, professor emeritus at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), where he was founding chair of the Department of Cognitive Science. He was also one of the founders of the Cognitive Science Society and has served as its chair and editor of its journal, Cognitive Science. His contributions to the field of HCI are rich of insight.
