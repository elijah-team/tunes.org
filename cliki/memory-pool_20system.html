Memory-Pool System

A resource: a client-server architecture for allowing multiple memory-allocation strategies to co-exist for the same environment by partitioning areas into pools. There is corresponding management for inter- and intra-pool references.

The project originated for Harlequin's Dylan system and is now published at Ravenbrook Limited (see below).
