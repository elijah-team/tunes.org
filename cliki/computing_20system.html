Computing System

A term for a system that allows men to do computations. Do not mix it up with a computer system which may be any kind of system based on a computer. A computing system is for dynamic human interaction, so that men may express their creativity, and adapt to an ever changing world.

It encompasses all of a computer's hardware and software that make it interact with the external world of human beings.
