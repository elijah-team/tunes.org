Abora

A Free implementation of the Xanadu model of hypertext system. It has four subprojects: Abora-White, a Java port of Udanax-Gold; the Dolphin Demo, a simple xanalogical hypertext system written in Dolphin Smalltalk; and Udanax-Gold2Java and Udanax-Gold2Dolphin, which are translators.
