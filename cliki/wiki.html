Wiki

Wiki is a class of collaborative hypertext authoring systems. Inspired by the original Ward's Wiki, many Wiki sites and software systems now exist in a variety of languages.

``MetaWiki'' content on CLiki is intentionally kept to a minimum, but interested readers are referred to MeatballWiki for further discussion.
