RPG

Report Program Generator, a family (RPG, RPG II, RPG III, RPG IV, RPG/400) of business oriented programming languages popular on IBM minicomputers, the System/3X series (S/34, S/36, S/38) of the late '70s - early '80s and successor series, the AS/400.
