Jean-Marc Fouet

Jean-Marc Fouet was a person who was a researcher in Artificial Intelligence.

He was a disciple of Jacques Pitrat and has been developing since his PhD thesis a knowledge-based inference engine named the Gosseyn machine.

  • His homepage (mostly in French): http://www710.univ-lyon1.fr/~fouet/
